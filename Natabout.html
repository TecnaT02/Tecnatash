FOUNDATION

The bakery and cafe as the name suggests was founded by a young lady by the name Natasha Barracks in the year 2006.She is the Head chef at the bakery and the sol owner of the bakery .She attained a Bachelors Degree in pastry from the University of Nairobi followed by a masters two years later from the same university.

But this is not how the bakery came about.The owner's grandma is the one who inspired her throughout her childhood as she would always feed her eith her delicious delicacies.The family recipe was pasted down to her mother who continued with baking in their family hence the source if the owners inspiration.

The recipes used by the owner are their personal Family Secret recipes passed down to her .The company has grown in the recent years and even managed to open a few more branches countrywide expanding their market reach.The company has not had any outbreaks with the law and has since kept its clean record.

Also competition wise the company has not received claims of injustice or mischief from the public and fellow competitors.As of now the company has opened a chance for classes in pastry and issuance of certificate and job opportunities within the country

As of now there are no job vacancies published yet so no applications are handled yet.As mentioned in the home page the working hours are between 5:00am-6:00pm EAT.

In the student training part the company take applications twice a year in the months of January and July it having a six months course.

The fee for the course is 40k and the requirements for application are the following:

  1. A chef uniform,full attair
  2. A certificate of KCSE
  3. An Identification Card
  4. Applicant must be neat and tidy

More details shall be provided upon admission for the classes.
